Diary of a Tech-Savvy Teen: Learning HTML and Sharing Life Experiences.

I'm Adelina, a 16-year-old teenager with an endless passion for technology and a burning desire to learn new things. And I want to share every moment of this journey with you! So, how did it all begin? Well, you might think that starting this blog to learn HTML might seem a bit strange, but let me explain. For some time now, I've been obsessed with the idea of understanding how websites work and how I can create one myself. With the support of a course called "The Girls Code: Breaking Barriers" by the ADFABER team, I managed to pave my way to HTML. But it's not just about HTML here. I want this space to be a place where we can connect and share experiences. So, alongside my journey into the world of technology, I'll also share fragments of my everyday life. From my passions and hobbies to my thoughts and feelings, everything will be on the table. So, if you're curious to learn more about HTML or if you just want to connect with a community of people who you can relate to, I invite you to join this journey. Let's learn and grow together, discover new horizons, and share the joys and challenges of life. So let's start this adventure together! I look forward to getting to know you and exploring the wonders of technology and life together!

I discover a new part of the world and myself, and each experience inspires and changes me in wonderful ways. While I often find myself seeking exotic and distant destinations, my love for Romania, my homeland, remains boundless. I love to explore every corner of this country full of history and natural beauty. The medieval castles, with their enchanting stories and impressive architecture, are among my favorite places. Each visit to a castle is like a journey through time, and each stone holds secrets centuries old. But despite my love for Romania, my heart also turns to Greece. I fondly remember my trips to Thassos, Paralia Katerini, Skala Potamia, and Skiathos, where I was immersed in the shimmering blue of the sea and wandered among picturesque streets and sunny beaches. Each island and each city was an adventure in itself, full of dreamy landscapes and memorable experiences. So, join me on this extraordinary journey through the world! Together, we'll discover the beauty and diversity of our world and create unforgettable memories along the way. Let's embark on this journey and explore together what the world has to offer!
